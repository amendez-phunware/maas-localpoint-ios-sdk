ILPLocationProvider Protocol Reference

Conforms to NSObject
Declared in ILPLocationProvider.h

Overview

A location provider acts as a store for locations deemed "relevant" by the SDK.

A location provider can be used by an application in two ways:

  1. synchronous - The provider can be queried for locations currently known to it.
  2. asynchronous - An application can register a {@link [ILPLocationListener](../Protocols/ILPLocationListener.html) listener} that will be notified when locations known are added to, deleted from or modified in the provider.

@version 1.1.0

Returns all locations filtered by the given filter and sorted by the given ordering.
